Rinon Gal

I am a Computer Science PhD student at Tel-Aviv University, under the supervision of Daniel Cohen-Or and Amit Bermano.

My main topics of research are generative models, both 3D and 2D, with a focus on reducing supervision through inductive bias.

When not working on my PhD, I work on Document Analysis problems and models which combine vision and language at WAY2VAT.
